Holdfast is now blocking a starter set of distracting sites. Here's the one thing that makes it different — and how to make it yours.

The deal: unblocking is the hard part

1

Wait

Hit a blocked site and the only way in is to start a cooldown — then wait it out. The timer keeps running even if you close the tab.

2

Write why

Type a real reason before you can unblock. No blank, thoughtless escapes.

3

Face history

See how often you've caved this week. Then decide. It grants a short pass, then re-blocks itself.

Your starter blocklist

Change the cooldown length, pass length, and see your history anytime from the toolbar icon → Settings & history. Your blocklist, history and browsing stay on your device.
